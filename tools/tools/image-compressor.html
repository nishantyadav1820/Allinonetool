How to Use

  1. Upload your image by dragging it into the upload area or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Adjust the quality slider to control compression level
  3. Optionally enable resizing to reduce dimensions
  4. Click "Compress" to process the image
  5. Preview the result and check the size reduction
  6. Click "Download" to save your compressed image

Features

  • Compress any image format (JPEG, PNG, etc.)
  • Adjustable compression quality
  • Optional image resizing
  • Preview before downloading
  • See compression statistics
  • Process images locally - no upload to server needed
